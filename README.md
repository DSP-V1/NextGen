## ✨ Welcome to NextGen V1!

We are an ambitious and passionate new project dedicated to bringing powerful and intuitive universal scripts to the Roblox community. Our vision for NextGen V1 is to develop a robust, feature-rich platform that enhances your experience across a wide array of Roblox games.

### 🚀 Our Mission

At NextGen, we believe in accessibility and community. We're not just building a script; we're building a foundation for future innovation. Our long-term goal is to achieve over **5,000 executions**, a testament to the trust and utility we aim to provide to our users.

### 🌐 Universal Approach

We are currently focused on developing a truly **universal script**. This means we're designing NextGen V1 with adaptability in mind, striving for broad compatibility and functionality that works seamlessly wherever you play.

### 🎨 Open-Source UI

Unlike many others, we believe that great UI should be shared. Our custom-designed UI library, crafted with a clean aesthetic and user-friendliness in mind, is currently **public and open for your use!** We're not "keepers" of nice-looking UI like Speed Hub X; we're contributors to the community. Feel free to explore, integrate, and build upon our UI to elevate your own projects.
